# Whats a team charter and why do we need it?
Clarity on teams mission and goals
Clear alignment for parters and stakeholders
Team accountability. No responsibility off-boarding. 

A team charter asks the following questions - 
Who are our customers?
Retailers - Supershops, car dealerships, Salons

What do we own and what are we responsible for?
Own the offline POS Webapp and we are responsible for building and maintaining it. 

What dont we do?
Design, Deploy

Why does our team exist?
To deliver a smooth User experience to our end users. 
To build features from scratch, 
To cater to stakeholder demands, 


How do we operate?
Team of 14 engineers 3 QAs. 
We receive paper prototypes and specifications from the design team and start architecting and developing it. 
QA team makes sure everything matches 1 on 1 with the design and specificaion. 
Its demo-ed in stakeholder meetings and then sent to Dev-Ops


How are we successful as a team?
We have managed to build this mega app from scratch in less than 10months. 

# How often should you revisit your charter?
1-2 times per year.
