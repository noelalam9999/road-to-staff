# Talk about a time when you had to do conflict resolution

Two engineers debating about Mongo and postgres and the team got divided. I pulled them aside and asked them to go to the root of the problem. I asked them to figure it among themselves if thats not possible and if I do have to call the shot I would ask them to create a comprehensive report with trade off analysis. Then I would make the decision. I would ask each team to counter the other teams points. Whoever holds up the best argument eventually wins.
